About Dikota

Innovating Everyday Life

Company Overview

Dikota was founded in 2014 with the vision of transforming lives through smart, sustainable, and user-friendly technology. Headquartered in Bangalore, India, we have grown into a global brand with a presence in over 25 countries.

Our dedicated team of engineers, designers, and innovators continuously strive to push boundaries and set new standards in the tech industry.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

  • Mission: To create innovative and sustainable technology solutions that enrich lives.
  • Vision: To be a global leader in smart living and digital transformation.
  • Core Values: Customer-first approach, Quality assurance, Innovation, Sustainability, Integrity.

Our Products

We offer a wide portfolio of smart and lifestyle products, including:

  • Smart Home Solutions: Smart bulbs, sensors, security cameras, voice-controlled hubs.
  • Wearable Tech: Smartwatches, fitness bands with health tracking features.
  • Personal Gadgets: Wireless earphones, power banks, charging stations.
  • Home Appliances: Air purifiers, smart fans, smart kitchen accessories.

All products are designed with minimalistic aesthetics and cutting-edge functionality.
