Our certified experts help you set up and customize smart devices in your home — from lights and thermostats to full security systems — all integrated with your voice assistants and mobile apps.

2. Product Customization & Integration

We provide tailored solutions that integrate Dikota devices with third-party platforms and systems for businesses, hotels, and smart offices, ensuring seamless control and automation.

3. Technical Support & Remote Assistance

Our support team is available 24/7 to help troubleshoot issues, update software, and assist you remotely to get your devices running smoothly, anytime, anywhere.

4. Business & Enterprise Solutions

Dikota offers scalable IoT and smart solutions for enterprises including energy monitoring, workspace automation, and employee wellness systems to improve productivity and efficiency.

5. AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts)

We offer affordable maintenance contracts that cover device health checks, firmware updates, priority service visits, and performance tuning for long-term reliability.

Whether you're a new user or a business client, we provide hands-on training sessions and digital workshops to ensure you get the most out of your smart technology investments.
